Indian Exotic Species

Indian Peafowl (Peacock)

India's national bird, known for its colorful, iridescent tail feathers displayed during courtship.

Indian Roller

A bright blue and brown bird often seen flying gracefully in open fields and grasslands.

Great Hornbill

A large bird with a striking yellow and black bill and a distinctive casque on its head.

Rose-ringed Parakeet

A vibrant green parakeet with a red ring around its neck, commonly seen in urban areas.

Malabar Pied Hornbill

A black-and-white hornbill with a large, colorful beak, found in the Western Ghats.

Scarlet Minivet

A small, brightly colored bird with vivid red and black plumage, typically found in forests.

Blue-headed Pitta

A colorful bird with blue, green, and red feathers, found in the Western Ghats' dense forests.

Indian Bustard

A large, endangered bird with a slender body and long neck, found in dry grasslands.

Painted Stork

A large wader with pink-and-white plumage and a long, curved bill, commonly found in wetlands.

Sunbird (Purple Sunbird)

A small, iridescent bird resembling a hummingbird, often feeding on nectar.

Black-necked Stork

A large wader with black-and-white plumage and red legs, found in marshes and riverbanks.

Yellow-footed Green Pigeon

A green pigeon with yellow feet, found in forests and urban areas.

Crested Serpent Eagle

A bird of prey with a distinctive crest, hunting mainly snakes and reptiles.

Keen's Wedge-billed Babbler

A small, elusive bird with a wedge-shaped bill, found in the Himalayan foothills.

Ashy Drongo

A sleek black bird with a forked tail, known for its acrobatic flight and sharp calls.
